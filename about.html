About Us

Our application, Parachute, will create ease during the often stressful and confusing process of investment. The problem that we are trying to solve for clients is to simplify financial planning and wealth management. Parachute aims to allow clients to upload financial documents online and choose their investment style based on their risk temperament. With the use of natural language processing, Parachute is able to construct a portfolio based on the client’s risk tolerance and offers automated investment according to the client’s financial information and preferences. Parachute can serve individual clients, as well as small financial planning offices looking to expand operations. Small financial planning offices can use Parachute to leverage the power of technology to better and more efficiently serve their clients. Individual do-it-yourself investors can find in Parachute the necessary tools to create their own investment portfolio without the need to meet a financial advisor in-person, allowing the clients the utmost convenience and flexibility.

Contact us for free one-hour financial planning consultation.
